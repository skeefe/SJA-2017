Welcome to St. John's Asquith!

Our goal is to bring the Good News of Jesus Christ to the people of Asquith, Mount Colah and Mount Kuring-Gai.

Come and join us at one of our three weekly services, and stay for coffee and a chat afterwards. We'd love to get to know you - visitors are always welcome!
